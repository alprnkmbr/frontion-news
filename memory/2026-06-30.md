Whimsical yet incisive visual commentary. Light, airy composition with plenty of negative space. Warm earth tones and soft pastels with one deep red accent. Horizontal 1200x675.

Mood: Witty, observational, understated — capturing the absurdity of geopolitics with a raised eyebrow rather than alarm.

CRITICAL ACCURACY RULES:

Exclude: No text, no watermarks, no detailed flags, no military hardware close-ups.
